# Task 2: Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective

The objective of this project is to create an interactive Power BI dashboard that transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through effective data visualization and storytelling.

---

## Dataset

**Dataset:** Sample Superstore

The dataset contains information related to customer orders, sales, profit, discounts, regions, product categories, and customer segments.

---

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Microsoft Excel

---

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

* Executive KPI Cards

  * Total Sales
  * Total Profit
  * Total Orders
  * Profit Margin
  * Average Discount

* Monthly Sales Trend

* Sales by Category

* Profit by Sub-Category

* Sales by Region

* Top 10 Customers by Sales

* Sales vs. Profit Analysis

* Interactive Filters (Region, Category, Segment, Order Date)

---

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales.
* West region contributed the highest revenue.
* Furniture generated strong sales but comparatively lower profit.
* Copiers delivered the highest profit among sub-categories.
* Top 10 customers account for a significant share of revenue.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Expand Technology product promotions.
* Review discount strategy for low-profit categories.
* Replicate West region sales strategies in other regions.
* Focus marketing efforts during high-sales periods.
* Strengthen customer loyalty programs for high-value customers.
